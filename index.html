Stay Safe in the Digital World

Learn about cyber crimes and protect yourself from online threats.

What is Cyber Crime?

Cyber crime refers to any illegal activity that is carried out using computers, digital devices, or the internet. In cyber crime, technology is either the target of the crime (such as hacking a system) or the tool used to commit the crime (such as online fraud). With the rapid growth of the internet, cyber crimes have increased significantly around the world. Criminals use advanced techniques to steal personal data, money, and sensitive information from individuals, businesses, and even governments. Cyber crime can affect anyone who uses digital devices, including students, professionals, and organizations.

Common Types of Cyber Crime:

  • Hacking
  • Phishing
  • Identity Theft
  • Ransomware Attacks
  • Online Fraud
  • Cyberbullying

How to Stay Safe:

  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Keep software updated
  • Use antivirus protection
